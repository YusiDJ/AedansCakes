About Aedan's Cakes and More


Crafting Art since 2016

Aedan’s Cakes and More is a well-known bakeshop in Camiling that serves almost all of our traditional Filipino delicacies along with other international recipes that will surely take your tastebuds for a ride!

The business kept growing and they always stick to their principle that no matter how many orders come in, every single one of them will be worth what they were paid for because the shop’s priority is the customer’s satisfaction. They make sure that the customer’s instructions and requests are met down to the last detail, taste and quality of the product is worth their buck, and that they are finished right on schedule so that there are no setbacks or inconveniences for both the customer and for the shop owners.

Aedan’s Cakes and More is here to satisfy you and all your celebratory needs! And even if there is no special occasion, they will make sure that anything you order from them will instantly make any day a special one in your homes.
